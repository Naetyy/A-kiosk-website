Our Background

Bobs started very small 10 yeaars ago with just one emplyee Mr Bob, the owner. This was way before Flo Filling Station had not yet grown to it current state. The main renovation of the Flo Filling Station along with the transformation of Bobs Kiosk occured 5 years ago and that was the start of real business. After the renovation we started seeing Bobs Kiosk become a one stop shop for most students from The National University of Science And Technology as well as passer bys along the Gwanda Road. We have witnessed Bobs Kiosk grow and we have also introduced a bottle store to suite the needs of those that enjoy alcohol and finally we stand at 3 employees.
